Stars and Stripes

Medium: Website

Date: December 2025

Stars and Stripes is a living archive built around the U.S. flag. This website transforms the flag into an interactive digital space where each star links to artistic interpretations of the flag and each stripe holds a thread of political or cultural relevance. The stars feature work like David Hammons’ African American Flag and Jasper Johns’ iconic renditions, showing how artists have reframed the flag across time. The stripes bring in real-world events, like political rallies or public incidents, including the 2024 Butler, PA shooting where the flag appeared in the background.
