About Me

I’m a multidisciplinary designer driven by a desire to make complex ideas more accessible.

With a dual-degree in Communication Design and Culture & Media from The New School, I approach each project with curiosity, balancing conceptual clarity with thoughtful execution.

My work is grounded in critical research, storytelling, and social impact, with a focus on civic engagement, community-building, and emerging digital tools that expand the possibilities of design.
