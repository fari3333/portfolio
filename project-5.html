Taste of Nostalgia

Medium: Digital Platform

Date: April 2025

Interactive AI-generated platform that invites users to build a personal memory landscape, piece by piece. The idea came from a single phrase—the taste of nostalgia—which instantly brought me back to cooking with my grandmother. From there, I began to imagine how memory, emotion, and visual style could be woven into a playful digital experience. The design draws on retro aesthetics, referencing 8-bit games and early interface graphics. To get the most accurate outputs, I broke the memory into smaller parts: characters, colors, objects, seasons, and names. Each element is generated separately, letting the user curate and assemble their own nostalgic scene. The result is a dreamy collage of mood and memory, shaped by AI and personal storytelling.
