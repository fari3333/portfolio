About the Black Panthers

Medium: Spiral bound book

Date: December 2024

This book is a visual and editorial exploration of the Black Panther Party, built from the text of its Wikipedia page. While the content came from an open source, I spent time shaping the visual direction to reflect the gravity, strength, and historical significance of the movement. .
