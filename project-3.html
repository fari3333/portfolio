About the Black Panthers

Medium: Spiral bound book

Date: December 2024

This book is a visual and editorial exploration of the Black Panther Party, built from the text of its Wikipedia page. While the content came from an open source, I spent time shaping the visual direction to reflect the gravity, strength, and historical significance of the movement.

The result is a carefully designed experience that honors the Panthers’ voice and vision. The layout and style draw inspiration from the Black Panther newspaper. I used bold typography, black-and-white imagery, and strong page rhythm to echo the urgency and clarity of the Party’s messaging. The book walks through the organization’s origins, rise, and core aims, creating an accessible and impactful record of revolutionary activism.
