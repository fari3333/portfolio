Asterisk Downtown

Medium: Digital Platform

Date: December 2024

Asterisk Downtown is a digital platform designed to support residents in Lower Manhattan as they navigate life with intermittent power and water. In response to local infrastructure challenges, this project focuses on clear communication, community coordination, and user-first design.

The platform includes real-time outage reports, a clean home dashboard, and a 24- and 48-hour schedule view to help residents plan ahead. A built-in task manager allows users to log actions, share responsibilities, and stay connected during disruptions. The visual language is simple and utilitarian, designed to reduce stress and offer clarity in times of uncertainty.
